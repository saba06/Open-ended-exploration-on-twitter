In this project we analysis communities formed around some prominent and influential people
form politics,,journilist/media,entertainment/telivision industry in twitter and the people
they follow on twitter (friends).

analysis on clustering:
we have used girvan newman algorithm to find communities.

analysis on classification:
we select gender as dimension for classification and find the number of male
and female members of community. we classify using names form census data of 1990 (http://www2.census.gov/topics/genealogy/1990surnames/)
